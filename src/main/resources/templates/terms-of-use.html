ONYX PDF is a robust PDF document management platform powered by Docker and backed by an Open Source platform. The website offers a wide range of functionality, from simple operations such as merging and splitting PDFs to advanced features such as compression, OCR and conversions.

The platform guarantees the highest privacy and security since it does not make outgoing calls for the purpose of tracking or keeping records. All files and PDF documents remain either on the user side, temporarily stored on the server during the execution of tasks, or within a transient file created specifically for the execution of the task. After the user downloads the file, or closes the Internet browser, it is immediately deleted from the server.

Cookies

Cookies are small files that your browser saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website (www.onyxpdf.com). This allows the Internet site to recognize your computer in order to offer you a personalized experience when moving through it, such as selecting certain language variants and other options of the web application itself. The cookies on our website are not aimed at spying on the user and do not follow everything the user does and are not malicious code or virus. They are also not related to unwanted messages (spam), they do not save your data - the website cannot access your personal information and files on your computer.

The cookies we use on this page are technical cookies, necessary for the functioning of this website, we cannot turn them off, and we do not need your consent to use this type of cookie. You can set your browser to block these cookies or send a warning about them, but in this case some parts of the site may not work.

Additionally, you can accept or reject some or all cookies by adjusting your browser settings. By searching Internet browsers, you can find information on how to change settings for some of the most commonly used Internet browsers: Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Microsoft Internet Explorer, Apple Safari, Opera. Some browsers allow you to browse the Internet in "anonymous" mode, limiting the amount of data placed on your computer and automatically deleting persistent cookies placed on your computer or mobile device when you end your browsing session. There are also many third-party applications that you can add to your browser to block or manage cookies. You can also delete cookies that were previously placed in your browser by selecting the option to delete browsing history and turning on the option to delete cookies.
